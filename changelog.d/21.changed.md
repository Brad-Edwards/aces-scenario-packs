### Changed

- Provenance schema advanced to the contract-v1 four-part ledger
  (`provenance.v1`), replacing the collapsed v0 `sources` + `scrubStatus` +
  `ownership` shape: a first-class `sources[]` ledger (stable id, kind, license,
  usage, attribution, used/excluded), a per-artifact-root **distribution class**
  (open / redistributable / internal-only / commercial-only / generated /
  consumer-specific — separate from runtime visibility), an all-true
  **content-safety attestation**, and a per-gate **publication-review** checklist,
  plus path-contained consumer overlays.

### Added

- Provenance-ledger validation gates in `aces_pack_tools`: content-safety
  all-true, publication-review blocked-status rejection, attribution
  completeness, source-reference integrity, and consumer-overlay containment /
  non-overlap — surfaced through `validate` when a `provenance.json` record is
  present. A shared root-overlap helper (`normalize_subtree` / `subtrees_overlap`)
  is promoted into `aces_pack_tools.schema` and reused by runtime-visibility and
  provenance.
