### Added

- Runtime-visibility artifact-tier contract: a `runtime-visibility` schema
  family classifying each pack artifact root as participant-visible, operator-
  only, oracle-only, or distribution-restricted — an axis orthogonal to and
  composable with artifact-boundary disposition, and a contract mapping rather
  than a mandated directory layout.
- Enforcement gates in `aces_pack_tools`: a participant-tier leak scan for
  secret-shaped and operator/oracle structural material, per-root path
  containment, tier-conflict detection, and a packaging boundary split that
  stages each tier into its own release root. Surfaced through `validate` when a
  `runtime-visibility.json` record is present and through a new `visibility`
  subcommand for release/CI re-scans.
