### Added

- Static, offline scenario-pack validation tooling (`tools/aces_pack_tools`):
  schema validation, leak/secret scan, runtime-profile portability, and
  release-record cross-check, behind an `argparse` CLI with sanitized,
  actionable findings and process exit codes.
- A credential-free consumer CI example under `examples/ci/` that external
  adopters can copy to validate their pack.
